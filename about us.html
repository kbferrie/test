We are a family of six trying to live our life full of experiences and out of the daily grind.  Follow us on as we prepare and take Serendipity on an extended voyage. 



Kevin
Captain/Deckhand/Oiler 

Kevin grew up on Merritt Island, FL and has been an avid boat from a young age. His experience is mainly on sportfishing boats and has dreams of outriggers and a live well onboard. He studied marine biology and physical oceanography at the US Coast Guard Academy and retired from the Coast Guard in 2015. While in the Coast Guard, Kevin worked in commercial vessel compliance, and has many years experience inspected commercial boats, from small excursion passenger boats to large oil tankers and cruise ships. He also has a masters degree in naval architecture from the University of Michigan.

Taking a year (or more) sabattical to cruise with the family has been a dream for many years and is most excited to share his love of the sea with his family.  

Stephanie 
First Mate and Admiral

Stephanie serves as the First Mate when sailing to a particular destination, but as Admiral in the overall management of the boat. She grew up in Amesbury, MA, and shares great passion for the ocean. She attended Florida Gulf Coast University, and is a labor and delivery nurse. She will also serve as the family doctor on board.

After undergoing treatment for cervical cancer in 2015-16, she has lost the patience to grind out life in hopes for a cushy retirement.  As a result, she has been the driving force to get us where we are today.
